Story line(1-100ep)

1. East Blue Saga (Episodes 1-61)
 Luffy begins his journey by gathering a crew and facing various foes in the East Blue.
Romance Dawn Arc (Ep 1-3)
Luffy sets sail from his hometown. He defeats the pirate Alvida and befriends Koby. Luffy recruits Roronoa Zoro, a swordsman, after defeating Marine Captain Morgan.
Orange Town Arc (Ep 4-8)
Luffy and Zoro meet Nami, a skilled thief who hates pirates. They fight Buggy the Clown, a pirate with the Chop-Chop Fruit. Nami temporarily joins them.
Syrup Village Arc (Ep 9-18)
The crew meets Usopp, a liar who dreams of becoming a brave warrior of the sea. They defeat Captain Kuro, a cunning pirate. Usopp joins the crew, and they get their first ship, the Going Merry.
Baratie Arc (Ep 19-30)
The crew reaches the floating restaurant Baratie. Luffy meets Sanji, a chivalrous cook. They battle Don Krieg, a powerful pirate. Luffy defeats Krieg, and Sanji joins the crew. Mysterious pirate Dracule Mihawk defeats Zoro, inspiring him to become stronger.
Arlong Park Arc (Ep 31-44)
Nami's tragic past is revealed; she was forced to work for Arlong, a fish-man pirate. Luffy fights Arlong to free Nami from his grasp. Arlong is defeated, and Nami officially joins the crew.
Loguetown Arc (Ep 45-53)
The crew arrives at Loguetown, where Gol D. Roger, the former Pirate King, was executed. Luffy is almost executed by Buggy but is saved by a sudden storm. Smoker, a Marine with Smoke-Smoke powers, tries to capture Luffy but is stopped by Monkey D. Dragon, Luffy's mysterious father.

2. Entering the Grand Line (Episodes 54-100)
The crew enters the Grand Line, a dangerous sea filled with powerful pirates.
Reverse Mountain Arc (Ep 61-63)
The crew crosses the Reverse Mountain and meets the giant whale Laboon. They learn about Crocus, a former member of Roger’s crew.
Whisky Peak Arc (Ep 64-67)
The crew reaches Whisky Peak, where bounty hunters attack them. Zoro defeats 100 bounty hunters alone. They meet Princess Vivi, who asks for their help to save her kingdom from Crocodile, a warlord of the sea.
Little Garden Arc (Ep 70-77)
They reach Little Garden, a prehistoric island. They meet two giant warriors, Dorry and Brogy. The crew fights Mr. 3, an agent of Baroque Works. Luffy and Usopp outsmart Mr. 3 and save the giants.
Drum Island Arc (Ep 78-91)
The crew lands on Drum Island in search of a doctor for Nami. They meet Tony Tony Chopper, a reindeer doctor who ate the Human-Human Fruit. Luffy defeats Wapol, the evil king of the island. Chopper joins the crew.
Alabasta Arc (Ep 92-100)
The crew finally reaches Alabasta, Vivi’s homeland. They prepare to fight Crocodile and his Baroque Works agents. Luffy meets Portgas D. Ace, his older brother. Ace is searching for Blackbeard, a rogue pirate. Luffy has his first battle with Crocodile but is defeated.
End of Episode 100:
Luffy is buried alive by Crocodile. The crew is preparing for their final battle in Alabasta to save the kingdom.

Story line(100-206ep)

Alabasta Arc (Episodes 100-130)
Luffy and the crew finally arrive in Alabasta, where the civil war between the royal army and Crocodile’s Baroque Works is at its peak. Luffy fights Crocodile multiple times and loses twice, but with his final attack, Gomu Gomu no Storm, he defeats him and saves Alabasta. The crew secretly departs after a tearful farewell to Vivi.
Post-Alabasta & Skypiea Setup (Episodes 131-143)
The Straw Hats meet Nico Robin, former Baroque Works assassin, who joins the crew. They encounter Bellamy and hear about the Sky Island, where a mythical city of gold is said to exist. Blackbeard is introduced as an upcoming threat.
Skypiea Arc (Episodes 144-195)
The crew reaches Skypiea, a floating island in the clouds, and gets involved in a war between the native Shandians and God Enel. Enel, wielding the Goro Goro no Mi (Lightning Devil Fruit), plans to destroy Skypiea using his flying ship, Maxim. Luffy, being made of rubber, is immune to Enel’s lightning and defeats him with Gomu Gomu no Golden Rifle. The lost city of Shandora is found, proving Mont Blanc Noland’s legend true.
G-8 Filler Arc (Episodes 196-206)
The crew crash-lands into the Marine G-8 base, and they must escape without getting caught.
